Sealed Briefing

"EXP: War Trauma" is a first-person psychological horror game that immerses players in the lingering horrors of war. You will take on the role of Krieger, a fictional World War II soldier struggling with Post Traumatic Stress Disorder (PTSD) after returning home from the battlefields of Europe. As Krieger, you'll experience his daily struggles with trauma, as well as the nightmarish visions that torment him while he sleeps.

The game's unique gameplay mechanics will take you through a series of increasingly terrifying nightmares, each one set in a different WW2 scenario. Each night will bring you closer to uncovering the truth about what happened to Krieger during the war and why the horrors of his experiences have never left him. From dealing with different enemies and creatures, to solving puzzles to uncover the truth. The game will offer an unparalleled experience of a soldier's mind haunted by the memories of war.
